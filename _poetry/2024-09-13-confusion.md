Why is it such a mouthful  
Confusion, a Syllable too many  
It leaves me exhausted  
It’s so hard to say  
So verbose a description  
There exists no word quite like it  
Yet today and Yesterday  
I can feel that word occupy it plenty  
Stuck within its haze  
It’s just too long a word to say
